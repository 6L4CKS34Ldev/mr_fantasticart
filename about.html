Rodney Isaacson — known as MR.FANTASTIC — is a self-taught digital illustrator whose work lives at the intersection of hip-hop culture, anime, and pop culture mythology.

What started nearly 5 years ago on an iPhone 6s — drawing with bare fingers — has grown into a bold, signature style built on Procreate and the iPad Pro. 17,000 followers later, the obsession hasn't slowed down.

From fan art that reimagines your favourite characters to commissions that turn real people into icons, every piece carries the same energy: whatever he does is fantastic.

How it works

THE
PROCESS

01
The Brief

You share your vision — a character, a concept, a vibe. The more detail the better, but even a rough idea is enough to start.

02
Sketch & Concept

A rough composition is laid out on the iPad. Poses, composition, and colour direction are locked in before any fine detail begins.

03
Line & Colour

Clean linework is built over the sketch, then the palette comes alive — neons, shadows, and that signature MF glow take over.

04
Delivery

Final high-resolution files delivered digitally. Print-ready if needed. Your world. Redrawn and ready to share.
